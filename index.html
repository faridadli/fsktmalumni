About Us

FSKTM Alumni is to connect you and your fellow graduates with a wealth of networking opportunities, professional development resources, programs, volunteer opportunities, faculty-led trips, and more. So, whether you are looking to build or expand your faculty community, reconnect with classmates, mentor students, engaged in lifelong learning, or stay informed on faculty news—the portal is here to keep you connected to all things University of Malaya.
